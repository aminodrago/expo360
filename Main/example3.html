EXPO360°

Spice up your e-commerce website!

Some features:

  • Smooth and attractive 360° view of your products
  • Zoom functionality to view details
  • Three kinds of hotspots
  • Autoplay and playback slider
  • Custom grabhand cursor
  • Configuration using XML with over 100 settings
  • External callbacks, for example: show front.

Works on mobile devices

(iPhone / iPad / android)

Present your products in 360°

Taking the product showcase on your website to the next level. By using Expo360°, your customer not only gets a better look and feel of the product that you are presenting, it also is a cool feature on your website that will blow everyone away!

Expo360° offers over 100 predefined XML settings to create custom interfaces matching your website's identity. You can insert a dynamic number of images, set a dynamic width and height of the window and add as many hotspots as you want.

12 different controls allow you to add all kinds of functionality varying from playback and zoom to adding external links and play-pause options.

To make it even more attractive, Expo360° is compatible with smartphones and touchpads, ensuring that you will reach your complete audience!
